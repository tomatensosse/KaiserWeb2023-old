More than just robots

Who is Kaiser?

Kaiser, named after the German word "Emperor", is a robotics team founded in 2014 within the German High School and participating in various competitions, mainly FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC).

In 2017, after meeting the FIRST foundation, Kaiser was restructured and became a social robotics club based on FRC values and STEM+A (Science, Technology, Engineering, Maths, Art) principles.

Our Achievements

Our Most Recent Achievements

Bosphorus Regional 2023 Impact

We received the First Impact Award at FIRST Bosphorus Regional on March 27-29, 2023 and qualified to go to the Houston World Championship in Houston.

The trainings we have given in the last three years as the Kaiser team, who have reached more than 10 million people through our organizations and the resources we publish, we are proud to represent our country in the finals where more than 500 teams from around the world will participate.

Dean's List Finalist Award

At the Istanbul Regional, on March 24-26 2023, with 48 different teams from 5 different countries, our team's co-captain Alisa Kurun received the Dean's List Award, given to students who achieve personal technical expertise and success while leading their teams and communities to raise awareness of FIRST and its mission, and qualified for the Houston World Championship as a Dean's List finalist.

Our Projects

Not content with just building robots, our team also sets an example to its surroundings with a wide range of social awareness and responsibility projects.

LEARNTECH

Learntech is the first and only technology-science education platform established by a robotics team. Learntech is a project consisting of 4 categories.

Just with the first catagory of our project, Learntech Semestr, we reached out to 250 students from 18 cities, 56 different schools.

KAISER TALKS

In our webinar series where we talk about Industry 4.0 and digital transformation, we bring Deutsche Schule Istanbul (German High School) graduates with DSI students together.

Kaiser Talks, is an active project that continues to host the leading names of the industry.

WOMEN IN SOCIETY

Based on the concept of gender equality, we are organizing “Women in Society” conferences. At these conferences, we discuss with our guests how equal opportunities can be better provided in all areas of society and how we can raise awareness on this issue.

FIRST LIBRARY

With our First Library project we translate all English resources of the FIRST Community into Turkish, German, French and Japanese so that more people can benefit from them.

At the same time we have translated the Wikipedia pages of First, FRC, FLL ve FLL Jr. into Turkish and German.

GIZ

Within the "Next Generation Science" project executed in cooperation with GIZ (German International Cooperation Agency), Ministry of Youth and Sports and Global Mind Association we are voluntarily mentoring an FLL team which was founded in Kahramanmaraş consisting of refugee and Turkish children.
